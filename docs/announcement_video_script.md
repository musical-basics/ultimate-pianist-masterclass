# Ultimate Pianist Masterclass — Announcement Video Script

## Target length: 3-5 minutes

---

## Beat 1: The Hook (30 seconds)

- "For years, you guys have been buying my sheet music — Moonlight Sonata Nightmare, Fur Elise Nightmare, Still Dre — and I love that."
- "But I also know that most of you who bought it never ended up learning it. Because honestly, it's really hard."
- "I'm fixing that."

---

## Beat 2: The Reveal (60-90 seconds)

- "I'm launching something called The Ultimate Pianist — a masterclass where I break down my most popular pieces into multiple difficulty levels."
- "So if you bought Moonlight Sonata Nightmare and couldn't play it, I'm going to teach you a simplified version that sounds beautiful and is actually playable. Then a medium version. Then the full arrangement."
- "You're going to learn my exact techniques, the way I actually think about these pieces, straight from me — the person who wrote them."
- "There are also 100 lessons already inside covering fundamentals and easier arrangements, so no matter where you are, there's something for you."

---

## Beat 3: The Keyboard Cross-Sell (30-45 seconds)

- "Some of you also know I've been building the DreamPlay keyboard. I still need about 15 more pre-orders to fund the prototype."
- "So here's what I'm doing. If you join the masterclass, whatever you spend, I'll match it dollar-for-dollar as a credit toward the DreamPlay keyboard."
- "But if you join the VIP waitlist right now for just $1, I'll DOUBLE your credits. Spend $197 on the masterclass, get almost $400 off the keyboard."

---

## Beat 4: The CTA (15-20 seconds)

- "The masterclass opens next week. Click the link below to join the VIP waitlist for $1."
- "The second you sign up, I'll send you the simplified sheet music for [Moonlight Sonata Nightmare / top piece] so you can start practicing right now."
- "Lock in your spot. I'll see you inside."

---

## Reminders for filming day

- One take mentality. Teach it like a private lesson, not a TED talk.
- You don't need to memorize this. Use these beats as a guide and talk naturally.
- Film at your piano with LED lights on — that's the visual your audience expects.
- Have your friend help with camera so you're not stressing about the setup.
