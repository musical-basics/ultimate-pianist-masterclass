# Ultimate Pianist Masterclass — Announcement Video Script (v2)

## Target length: 3-5 minutes

---

Hi, I'm Lionel. I'm a concert pianist — I've performed at Carnegie Hall and Kennedy Center — and my YouTube channel has over a million subscribers. You might know me from my Moonlight Sonata Nightmare videos, which have a combined 30 million views on Youtube.


## Beat 1: The Hook (30 seconds)

- "For years, you guys have been buying my sheet music — Moonlight Sonata Nightmare, Fur Elise Nightmare, Still Dre — and I'm so happy about that."
- "But I also know that most of you who bought it never ended up learning it. Because honestly, it's really hard."
- "And it's not your fault. Sheet music alone doesn't teach you how to actually play something. It doesn't give you the technique, the practice strategy, or the context you need to get there."

---

These are the pieces you've heard on my channel — Moonlight Sonata Nightmare, Fur Elise Nightmare, Still Dre — but broken down into Easy, Medium, and Full versions so you can actually learn them."

By the time you can play the full version, you haven't just learned a song. You've become a fundamentally stronger pianist."
"And there are already 100 lessons inside, all featuring my own arrangements — from Twinkle Twinkle Little Star all the way up to Beethoven's Fifth. So no matter where you are right now, you'll never have to play a boring piece to get better."

## Reminders for filming day

- One take mentality. Teach it like a private lesson, not a TED talk.
- You don't need to memorize this. Use these beats as a guide and talk naturally.
- Film at your piano with LED lights on — that's the visual your audience expects.
- Have your friend help with camera so you're not stressing about the setup.
- The key reframe: your pieces are the VEHICLE, not the destination. The destination is becoming a stronger pianist overall.
